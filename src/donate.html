  • Promote an integration program

How much does a Lasso cost you?

Lazsos is an organization of general interest of an educational nature, it is therefore possible to obtain a tax reduction of 66% of the amount of donations. The reduction applies up to 20% of taxable income. For example: if you make a Lasso of €30, you can obtain a deduction of €19.80. Your Lasso will only cost you €10.2
